`. = Jump to exact spot in last modification line
<Ctrl-O> = Retrace your movements in file (backward)
<Ctrl-I> = Retrace your movements in file (forward)
:ju(mps) = List of your movements {{help|jump-motions}}
:history = List of all your command
m{a-z} = Marks current position as {a-z}, eg: ma marks as "a"
'a = Move to first character of 'a-marked line
y'a = Yank from current position to (incl.) 'a-marked line
